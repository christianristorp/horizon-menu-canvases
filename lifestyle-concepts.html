Lifestyle Concept Penetration by Segment

How often each customer segment's actual recipe selections match each lifestyle concept. Concepts overlap (one recipe can match several), so values are independent penetration rates and do not sum to 100%. Read top-down: the overarching view is the big picture, the collapsed view is the 7-segment planning grain, and the detailed view exposes the raw segment pairs.

Methodology

What this measures. Every row is a customer segment. Each concept percentage is the penetration: the share of that segment's recipe selections (individual recipes chosen into a box) that match the concept. Because a recipe can carry several concepts at once, penetrations are independent and do not sum to 100%.Selection universe. Recipe selections from facts_recipes_ordered_enriched, joined to the segmentation summary on customer + country. Scope: DK + SE, weeks 2025-W31 to 2025-W52, Standard core recipes only (slot_type = Recipe, excluding Modular Recipes), counting only recipes actively kept or swapped in. 1,726,147 segmented selections.Concept definitions (Signaletics Master). Super Quick = tag Super-quick; Assemble & Bake = tag Prepped-in-10; Quick Meals = hands-on + hands-off cooking time of exactly 20 minutes; Low Calorie = tag Under-650-calories; High Protein = tag High-protein; High Veg = tag High-veggie; Family-Friendly = tag Family-friendly or Child-friendly; Parents to Kids = tag Parents-to-kids; BYOP = tag Build-your-own-plate; Bestseller and Customer Favourite come from the recipe label (Customer Favourite = recipe score above 3.5).Index (View B). Each cell is the segment's penetration divided by the menu-wide average penetration. 1.00 = on par with the menu; above 1 over-indexes (green), below 1 under-indexes (orange). Menu-wide averages: Super Quick 16.2%, Assemble & Bake 4.9%, Quick Meals 21.3%, Low Calorie 20.2%, High Protein 16.1%, High Veg 13.4%, Family-Friendly 35.8%, Parents to Kids 2.5%, BYOP 1.9%, Bestseller 7.6%, Customer Favourite 14.7%.Customer share. "Cust. share" = distinct customers in that segment as a % of the total DKSE base (43,840). At Layer 3 a second figure, "reach", sums the collapsed-segment shares that contain each main segment (double-counts overlap customers, so it shows total reach).Concepts not plotted: Born Veggie, Kids' Favourites and Easy Prep are currently undefined concepts (placeholders for future attention). Nordic Flavour and Cuisine Spotlight are 2026 menu-label concepts with no selections in the 2025 segmentation window.
