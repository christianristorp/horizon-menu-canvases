Methodology

What this measures. Every row is a customer segment. The six protein percentages show what share of that segment's recipe selections (individual recipes chosen into a box, not customers and not boxes) fell into each protein group. Percentages are within-segment and sum to 100% across the six groups in each row.Selection universe. Recipe selections from facts_recipes_ordered_enriched, joined to the segmentation table on customer + country. Scope: DK + SE, weeks 2025-W31 to 2025-W52 (the H2-2025 segmentation window), Standard core recipes only (slot_type = Recipe, excluding Modular Recipes), counting only recipes the customer actively kept or swapped in. One row in the source = one recipe in one customer's box in one week.Protein grouping. The 9 raw categories are collapsed to 6: Fish & Seafood = Fish + Shellfish; Vegetarian = Veggie + Vegan + Plantbased; Beef, Chicken, Pork and Beef and Pork are unchanged.Customer share (two definitions). "Cust. share" = distinct customers in that segment as a % of the total DKSE base (43,840). At Layer 3 a second column, "Reach (roll-up)", sums the collapsed-segment shares that contain each main segment — e.g. Family reach = Family+Convenience (40.3%) + Family+Healthy (18.2%) + Family (9.6%) = 68.0%. Reach double-counts customers in overlapping segments, so it shows total addressable reach rather than an exclusive share.

The three layers

Layer 3 — Overarching (main segment). Customers grouped by main segment, with Vegetarian + Pescatarian merged into Veg/Pesc. The protein percentages here are customer-weighted across the collapsed segments that roll up into each main segment, so they correlate with the reach figure rather than being a separately computed aggregate.Layer 2 — Collapsed (7 segments). The planning grain. Overlapping pairs are merged regardless of order (Family+Convenience = Convenience+Family). Each customer appears once, so customer shares sum to ~100%. Other is shown for reference but is excluded from menu slot allocation.Layer 1 — Super-detailed (main x secondary). The raw segment pairs before collapsing. "None" means no secondary segment. Pescatarian and Vegetarian are shown separately here (not yet merged into Veg/Pesc). Each customer appears once.
